How it works

Do you want to reduce the food waste by making the most of the food you currently have in your fridge?

How often do you struggle with finding the right recipe based on the ingredients in your kitchen?

Using the recipe finder below you will get a great list of the recipes based on your ingredience list. Witch one click you will get the results from the top recipe websites and blogs available on the Web.

Simply write the list of items from your fridge or kitchen cupboard (best results come when selecting 4-5 ingredients) and browse the recipes.
